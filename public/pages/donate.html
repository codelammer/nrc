How is my donation used?

NRC 2021 has a goal of raising US$4,610,000 towards strengthening rehabilitation in Kenya’s health System in line with the Rehab2030 initiative.

How is my donation secure?

A multi-prolonged strategy will be applied to attain the overall fundraising goal and intended purpose. Long-term program funding will be informed by rehabilitation needs identified in the course of stakeholder engagements before and during the conference.
